

# SG Benefits Consulting Website

A professional website for SG Benefits Consulting (Pty) Ltd — a company that helps individuals recover unclaimed pension funds, provident funds, insurance claims, and other savings. The site will use **dark green, light green, and white** brand colours with the provided logo.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero section** with a compelling headline about recovering unclaimed funds (R90 billion+ in unclaimed monies), a call-to-action button leading to the claims inquiry form
- **Key statistics** section (e.g., R90B+ unclaimed, years of experience, claims processed)
- **Services overview** cards for the 5 claim types: Pension & Provident Funds, Guardian Fund, Insurance Claims, Injury on Duty (IOD), and Other Claims
- **How it works** — simple 3-step process (Contact Us → We Investigate → You Get Paid)
- **Trust indicators** — founder's 22+ years of experience, company registration details

### 2. About Page
- Company background and story (founded 2015 by Steve Ngobenza)
- Founder's extensive experience in pension fund administration, GEPF, financial advisory
- Mission: "Giving Back the Service of Pension Fund"
- Vision and values from the business plan
- Key partnerships (Fund administrators, Insurance companies, GEPF, Guardian Fund, etc.)

### 3. Services Page
- Detailed descriptions of each claim type:
  - **Pension & Provident Fund Claims** — recovery from GEPF and private sector funds
  - **Guardian Fund Claims** — claims from the Master of High Court
  - **Insurance Claims** — long-term insurance and related recoveries
  - **Injury on Duty (IOD)** — workplace injury compensation claims
  - **Other Claims** — medical aid savings and related benefits
- Fee structure overview (15% service fee, 2.5% cost-to-client fee)

### 4. FAQ Page
- Common questions about unclaimed benefits, the claims process, required documents, timelines, fees, and eligibility

### 5. Contact / Claims Inquiry Page
- **Simple claims inquiry form** (no login required) with fields for:
  - Full name, ID number, phone, email
  - Type of claim (dropdown selection)
  - Former employer name
  - Brief description of the claim
  - Document upload capability (ID copy, proof of employment)
- Company contact details (phone, email, address)
- Operating hours

## Design & Layout
- **Header**: Logo + navigation menu with mobile hamburger menu
- **Footer**: Contact info, quick links, social media icons, company registration details
- **Colour scheme**: Dark green (#004d40), light green (#66bb6a), white (#ffffff)
- **Responsive** design for mobile and desktop
- **Professional and trustworthy** feel appropriate for financial services
